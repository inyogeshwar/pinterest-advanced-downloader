Pinterest Advanced Downloader

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2025

Overview

Pinterest Advanced Downloader is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our browser extension handles information.

What Information We Collect

Pinterest Advanced Downloader does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information about you or your browsing habits. We do not track your activity or send data to any external servers.

Local Storage

The extension stores your preferences (such as download settings) locally in your browser using the browser's built-in storage API. This information never leaves your device and is used solely to remember your preferred settings between browser sessions.

Website Access

Pinterest Advanced Downloader needs access to pinterest.com websites to function. This access is used exclusively to identify images and videos on Pinterest pages and add download functionality to them. The extension does not modify or collect the content you view on Pinterest.

Downloads

When you download images or videos using our extension, these files are saved directly to your device using the browser's download API. No record of your downloads is kept beyond what your browser normally records in its download history.

Third-Party Services

Pinterest Advanced Downloader does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page and notify users through the extension's update process.
